Credits

WindFlow is the result of the work of people at the Parallel Programming Models group (PPMs) at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Pisa, Italy. The library is still under development to improve it and to provide additional features. The main authors or contributors are listed below:

  • Gabriele Mencagli (Assistant Professor), main maintainer
  • Massimo Torquati (Assistant Professor), FastFlow's main maintainer
  • Tiziano De Matteis (Research Fellow), contributor
  • Alessandra Fais (Master student), contributor
  • Elia Ruggeri (Master student), contributor
  • Andrea Cardaci (Master student), contributor
  • Cosimo Agati (Master student), contributor

For any information regarding the library, please send and email to Gabriele Mencagli.
